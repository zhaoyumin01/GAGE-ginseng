## 2. Dataset Overview / 数据集简介

This dataset is constructed for the research of fine-grained age estimation of forest-grown ginseng, specifically supporting the paper **"GAGE: Graph-Augmented Gradient Estimator for Forest-Grown Ginseng Age Estimation"**.

* **Content:** High-resolution images of forest-grown ginseng.
* **Age Range:** 15 to 30 years (Continuous age labels).
* **Scale:** Total 2,433 images (Balanced subset for training).
* **Source:** National Ginseng and Antler Product Quality Inspection and Detection Center & Market Collection.

本数据集是为林下山参细粒度年限估计研究而构建的，主要用于支持论文 **"GAGE"** 的实验。数据集包含高质量的林下山参图像，覆盖 15-30 年的生长周期。所有样本均由资深专家进行鉴定和标注。
