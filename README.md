## 2. Dataset Overview 

This dataset is constructed for the research of fine-grained age estimation of forest-grown ginseng, specifically supporting the paper **"GAGE: A Graph-based and Prototype-Guided Framework for Fine-Grained Visual Ginseng Age Estimation"**.

* **Content:** High-resolution images of forest-grown ginseng.
* **Age Range:** 15 to 30 years (Continuous age labels).
* **Source:** National Ginseng and Antler Product Quality Inspection and Detection Center & Market Collection.
